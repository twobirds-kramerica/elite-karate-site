Our Story

A Dojo Built on Respect

"At Kirk's Elite Karate Club, we specialise in Shorin-ryu Jiu-Jitsu, honouring traditional martial arts while adapting to the modern world."

Founded by Sensei Trevor Kirk, our dojo is a place where students of all ages and abilities learn discipline, respect, and self-defence. Whether you are four years old taking your first stance or an adult looking for a new challenge, you belong here.
