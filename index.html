"Karate Family"

Our Story

Kirk's Elite Karate Club was founded with a simple mission: to provide high-quality martial arts instruction in a humble, grounded environment. Led by Sensei Trevor Kirk, we focus on traditional Okinawan Shorin-Ryu—a style known for its natural movements and practical application.

Our club is a proud member of the Canadian Jiu Jitsu Council, maintaining rigorous standards for over two decades of instruction.

Brave Hearts (Kids Program)

Our kids' program is designed to turn high energy into high achievement. We focus on:

  • Discipline and Respect
  • Focus and Concentration
  • Self-Defense & Bully Prevention

Adult Shorin-Ryu & Jiu-Jitsu

Whether you are looking for fitness, stress relief, or traditional mastery, our adult program offers a balanced approach. We teach fluid, circular movements that are effective for students of all ages and fitness levels.
