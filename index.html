Our Story

Founded by Sensei Trevor Kirk, Kirk's Elite Karate Club has been a staple of the St. Thomas community since 2010. We specialize in traditional Okinawan Shorin-Ryu and Jiu-Jitsu.

Our focus is on the "Brave Heart"—building discipline, focus, and respect in every student.
